Our Work Process

Transparency and Precision in Every Step

01

Submit Inquiry

Client submits inquiry via website, app, or phone. Provide property details and survey type. We review and provide a feasibility check.

02

Team Allocation

Appropriate field team is assigned based on project type, location, and timeline. We coordinate the site visit according to your availability.

03

On-Site Survey

Expert team visits site with advanced instruments (Total Station, GNSS) and collects precise measurements and boundary data.

04

Data Processing

Raw field data is verified, processed using CAD software, and compiled into final high-accuracy drawings and detailed reports.

05

Report Delivery

Final survey documents delivered digitally or physically. We walk you through the findings if needed. Project successfully closed.
